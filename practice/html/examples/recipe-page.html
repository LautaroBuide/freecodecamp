Homemade Pizza Recipe

This is a page for learning how to make a pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of 000 flour
  • 25 grams of fresh yeast bread
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 cup of warm water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl or cup, combine the yeast, half a cup of warm water, and the tablespoon of sugar. Mix well until bubbles form, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise for about 10 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, place the flour and mix in the salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the half-cup of water containing the yeast. Add the remaining warm water. Bring all the ingredients together with both hands to form a dough. Gradually drizzle in the oil while continuing to knead.
  3. Let the pizza dough rest for about 15 minutes, then knead it again on a floured surface. Divide the dough into two equal-sized balls.
  4. Roll out the dough into a circle, place it on a pizza pan, and let it rest in a warm place until it rises—about 15 minutes.
  5. Place in a hot oven for six minutes. This duration is important to ensure the dough is thoroughly cooked.
  6. Add a generous spoonful of tomato sauce, spread it out, and place in the oven for another two or three minutes. You can add mozzarella, cooked ham, onion, and even a few tomato slices, depending on your preference. The recipe is done—time to enjoy!
